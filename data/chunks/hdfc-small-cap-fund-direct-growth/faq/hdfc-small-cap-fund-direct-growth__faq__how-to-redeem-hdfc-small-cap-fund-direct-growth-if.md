## FAQ

**Q: How to Redeem HDFC Small Cap Fund Direct Growth?If you want to sell your HDFC Small Cap Fund Direct Growth holdings, go to your holding on the app or web and simply click on it. You will get two options - redeem & invest more; click on redeem and enter your desired amount or if you wish to redeem the entire holding amount then select the 'redeem all' checkbox.**

Can I invest in SIP and Lump Sum of HDFC Small Cap Fund Direct Growth?You can select either SIP or Lumpsum investment of HDFC Small Cap Fund Direct Growth based on your investment objective and risk tolerance.
